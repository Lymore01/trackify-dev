## What is Trackify?

<Spacer />
Trackify is your high-impact platform for **link management**, real-time **analytics**, and **event infrastructure**. Designed for the modern developer era, Trackify empowers you to organize your digital assets into modular **App Containers**.

Whether you’re an individual developer, a growing business, or a large-scale platform, Trackify helps you:
<Spacer />

## Core Capabilities

<Spacer />

- **Modular App Containers**: Organize your links and webhooks into logical projects for better management.
- **Real-time Event Infrastructure**: Receive instant notifications via Webhooks whenever your links are interacted with.
- **Granular Geo-Intelligence**: Capture precise geographic data, device types, and network info for every click.
- **Developer-First SDK**: Integrate seamlessly into your own applications with our lightweight, powerful SDK.
- **Actionable Insights**: Gain deep visibility into link performance with cinematic dashboards and detailed analytics.
  <Spacer />

## TL;DR;

<Spacer />

With Trackify, you can automate link creation, monitor your shop or app’s performance, and make data-driven decisions, all from a single, intuitive dashboard.
