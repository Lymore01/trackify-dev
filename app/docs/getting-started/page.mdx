# Getting Started

<Spacer />

Welcome to the Trackify documentation!

<Spacer />

This **Getting Started** section will guide you through setting up your Trackify account, creating your first link, and integrating Trackify into your workflow.

<Spacer />

## 1. Create Your Account

<Spacer />

- Visit [trackify.dev](https://trackify.dev) and sign up for a free account.
- Log in to access your cinematic dashboard.

<Spacer />

## 2. Initialize an App Container

<Spacer />

Trackify organizes everything inside **App Containers**. Before creating links, you need a container.

- In your dashboard, click on **Create App**.
- Give your app a unique name (e.g., "My Web Shop" or "Marketing Q1").
- This container will hold all your links, webhooks, and analytics for this specific project.

<Spacer />

## 3. Create Your First Link

<Spacer />

- Once inside your App Container, click on **Create Link**.
- Enter the destination URL you want to track.
- Click **Save** to generate your trackable short link.

<Spacer />

## 3. Explore Analytics

<Spacer />

- Click on any link in your dashboard to view detailed analytics.
- Track clicks, referrers, locations, and device types in real time.
- Use filters to analyze performance over different time periods.

<Spacer />

## 4. Integrate with Your App or Website

<Spacer />

- Access your API key from the **Developer** section in your dashboard.
- Use our SDKs or REST API to programmatically create and manage links.

<Spacer />

You’re now ready to start using Trackify to manage and track your links efficiently!
<Spacer />
